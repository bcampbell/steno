Bulk tag

Bulk tagging is controlled by csv files with two columns. The first column is a query to execute. The second column is the tag to add to all articles matching that query.

eg: 
query,add
headline:grapefruit,citrus
headline:lemon,citrus
headline:orange,citrus
Upon uploading the control file, its queries are run and its tags are applied.
